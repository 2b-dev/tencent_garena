PROGRAM A. ZIPLINE A.溜索

Face your fear of heights by going on a zipline from tree to tree and also walking across a sky bridge
重新回忆起被高度支配的恐惧，体验原始丛林中的溜索与高桥。

PROGRAM B. PHI PHI ISLAND AND SNORKELING B.皮皮岛和浮潜

Go snorkeling with fishes in the beautiful ocean and have a relaxing time by the beach
与美丽的海洋生物来次亲密接触，一次放松的近海之旅。

PROGRAM C. THAI MASSAGE AND SPA C. 泰式按摩和SPA

Have a nice relaxing 2-hour long Thai massage to calm your body and a nice walk around Phuket Old town after that
享受属于你和泰式按摩的2小时平静时光，在按摩之后漫步普吉岛老城区

PROGRAM D. SIGHTSEEING & SHOPPING CITY TOUR 计划D.观光和购物城旅游

Explore Phuket Old Town and the beautiful temples around it
探索普吉岛旧城区和周围的美丽寺庙，享受自然风光然后买买买

PROGRAM E. WAKEBOARD 方案E水上滑板

Feel the force of the water and the wind in your hair as you surf on a wakeboard
挑战极限，水上滑板乐园了解一下。

PROGRAM F. RAFTING 方案F. 峡谷漂流

Paddle your way down a gushing river with your friends and experience the adrenaline rush
激流直下，在喷薄汹涌的河道上体验漂流的快感，肾上腺素飙起来。

PROGRAM G. HYPE CRUISE 游艇

Party and relax on the luxury yacht that sails along the shores of the beach
随着豪华游艇一起在沿海放松或趴体，享受宁静的海上风光。

PROGRAM H. FLOW HOUSE & JET SKI 人造滑浪区＆水上摩托艇

Surf up in the Flow House and ride alongside the ocean waves on a jet ski
在人造滑浪区体验冲浪的感觉，或是乘坐水上摩托艇沿海踏浪。
